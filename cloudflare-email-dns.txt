# DNS Records for Cloudflare Email

## Required MX Records (Auto-added by Cloudflare)
# These will be automatically added when you enable Email Routing

## SPF Record (Add this manually)
Type: TXT
Name: @
Content: "v=spf1 include:_spf.mx.cloudflare.net ~all"

## DMARC Record (Recommended)
Type: TXT
Name: _dmarc
Content: "v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@isolated.tech"

## DKIM (Will be provided by Cloudflare)
# Cloudflare will provide DKIM records when you enable Email Routing
